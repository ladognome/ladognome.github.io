My Research

What Do I Like?

 
I'm interested in dialog systems, computational creativity, interactive narrative, and applied speech technologies: prosody and its semantic implications, among other things.

What Am I Working On?

 
I'm making an AI that makes up a new story with you. Specifically, I am working on creating a computational improvisational system where the agent co-creates a story along with the user in real time. My advisor is Dr. Mark Riedl.
